What is DialectAI?

DialectAI is a powerful AI-based translation tool designed to recognize and translate regional Telugu dialects like Chittoor and East Godavari into English. It uses advanced speech-to-text, dialect mapping, and machine translation to ensure accurate, contextual translation from dialect to Standard Telugu and then to English.
